App Info​

#Description
1App Info swimlane category
2Parental Controls On/Off toggle, click switch to hide/show explicit content
3FAQ card, click to view FAQ content
4Contact Us card, click to view Contact Us info
5About card, click to view About info

 

info
  • Explicit songs will not be displayed when parental controls are ON unless they are in the Sing it again or Favorites categories where they will be displayed as a generic, unclickable, locked card without cover art nor labels.

Sing it again​

#Description
1Row category title
2Previously played song card(s), from most recent to oldest, click to view Song Info
3Ghost card placeholder
4More button, click to view Sing it again grid screen
info
  • The Sing it again category is also displayed on the Home screen if there is at least one song in the history
  • The ghost card placeholders are replaced by song cards as the row is populated with more songs
